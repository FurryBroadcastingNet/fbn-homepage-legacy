Frequently Asked Questions

What is Furry Broadcasting Network (FBN)?

We’re a collective of artists, musicians, and gamers who want to do our best to help other members of the furry community.

How do I contact for more information?

We’re a collective of artists, musicians, and gamers who want to do our best to help other members of the furry community.

How do I submit my work to be featured?

Please send your request to info@furrybroadcasting.net with the subject of “Request to be featured”. It will take up to 3 business days to send you an email back saying if you have been approved or denied. If denied we will tell you what is wrong and you can send us another email with the corrected issue.

How do I become a partner?

Send us an email to info@furrybroadcasting.net with the title “Become a partner”. Within 3 days you will have an email back with either or accept or reject along with further instructions.

How do I apply for staff?

Please click the following link, Apply for FBN Staff Position . Only apply if you have time available to dedicate to tasks issued to you. All our staff are volunteers, so we wont take over your life but we do expect that things be done in some kind of time frame.
